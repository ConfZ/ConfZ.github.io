Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Tianjin University, China (天津大学). Previously, I received my Master of Engineering and Doctor of Engineering degrees in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohong Li, Prof. Guangquan Xu, and Prof. Xiaofei Xie,. My research interests include program analysis, software testing, large model security, and intelligent software engineering.
